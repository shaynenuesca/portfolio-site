About

Shayne Nuesca is a nationally recognized multimedia storyteller and narrative strategist working at the intersection of digital media, culture, preservation and social change. Born in San Fernando, La Union, Philippines and raised in Anchorage, Alaska, she draws on her experiences as a 1.5-generation Ilokana American to inform her practice, fostering spaces where people like her are seen, heard and valued. Her approach centers on themes of returning and reclamation, guiding people, brands and organizations to stay grounded in their values and principles in all aspects of their work.
